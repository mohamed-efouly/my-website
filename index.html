About Us

Loving Homes is a premium dog hotel based in Hong Kong, offering a safe, comfortable, and fun environment for every dog.

Our facilities include a grooming parlor and spa, luxury rooms with heating, cooling, and TVs, a chauffeur collect-and-return service, and secure outdoor paddocks.

We also provide woodland walks, private grass areas, an indoor play barn, and 24-hour on-call veterinary services — ensuring every dog feels happy, relaxed, and well cared for.

At Loving Homes, we believe that every dog deserves to be loved and pampered, just like family.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make every stay special. We aim to combine comfort, safety, and joy — so every dog enjoys their time as much as their owners enjoy peace of mind.
